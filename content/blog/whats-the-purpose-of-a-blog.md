Finding a purpose to keep writing here, I've found some unexpected inspiration along the way.
<!-- more -->
I've been thinking about how to keep this website and blog current, I don't post as many new projects as I used to, often because I'm no longer on projects in the same capacity.

Recently I came across a website [Terrible Software](https://terriblesoftware.org/) that I have enjoyed reading. It came across from an interesting perspective and I found myself interested in a lot they had to say.

This made me think, maybe this is the ideal thing to write about here. My experiences moving into a position of leadership and some of my thoughts along the way. We're all opinionated about how to do things and I believe with an open mind and curiosity it's always an interesting read into how others tackle similar scenarios 😄
