About Me

I am a mid-weight CG/VFX Generalist with a current focus on using Houdini in production. With over 7 years of professional industry experience across the UK and Australia, I am currently based in London.

I have held various positions on a wide range of projects in Architecture, TVC and Film.

My aim is to work on projects that push the boundaries of my skills, enable me to learn and grow, work hard both as a team player and individually while striving to produce something everybody can be proud of.
